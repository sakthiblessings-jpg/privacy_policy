Sakthi – Global Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Welcome to Sakthi, a period cycle tracking and health record management application. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your most personal health data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information in compliance with global privacy standards, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and standard medical data privacy practices.

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect varying levels of data, distinguishing between personal identifying data and sensitive health data:

  • Account Information: Your email address, used solely for account authentication, customer support, and deletion requests.
  • Health & Biometric Data: Menstrual cycle dates, symptoms, moods, and any other specific health records you voluntarily enter into the application.
  • Device & Usage Data: Basic diagnostic information necessary for the functioning and improvement of the app (e.g., OS version, device type).

2. Medical Privacy & Data Encryption

We believe your health data belongs entirely to you. Sakthi adheres to stringent, global privacy standards designed for medical applications.

  • Database Encryption: All health data, cycle logs, and personal information are encrypted at rest within our database and in transit using advanced, industry-standard cryptographic protocols.
  • Anonymization: Where possible, identifiers are separated from your health records to prevent unauthorized tracking.

3. Strict No Third-Party Sharing Policy

We do not, and will never, sell, rent, monetize, or share your personal data or health records with any third-party advertisers, data brokers, or marketing agencies. Your data is used exclusively to provide you with insights into your own health cycle and to maintain the functionality of the Sakthi app.

4. Your Data Rights & Account Deletion

Under global privacy laws (including GDPR and CCPA), you have the absolute right to access, modify, restrict processing of, and request the deletion of your personal data.

The "Right to be Forgotten" (Account Deletion)

If you wish to terminate your account and erase all associated data permanently, follow these steps:

Please send an explicit deletion request from your registered email address to:

sakthi.blessings@gmail.com

Upon receiving your email, our team will review the request. Within a maximum of thirty (30) days, your account, personal data, and all health records will be irreversibly erased from our encrypted database and backups. We will send a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

5. Global & Regional Compliance

  • European Economic Area (GDPR): We act as the data controller. Our legal basis for processing your data is your explicit consent provided upon account creation. You may withdraw this consent at any time via the deletion process above.
  • California Residents (CCPA): We do not "sell" your personal information. You have the right to request a summary of the data we hold about you by contacting us at the email provided above.

6. Children's Privacy

Sakthi is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to remove that information immediately.
